RE:FUTURE

4/22/2006

As we near the end of the worst 4 months of my life, I’ve been thinking a lot about what my purpose is in this world. Aside from questioning my stability from here on out, I have no idea what I want to do as a career, I just know I need to help people. I feel so responsible for the way Mike and Sam feel, and everything I’ve thought could help the world, like reducing emotions to binaries, isn’t what people need. We need real permanence. I can’t prove a single goddamn aspect of my life has happened during the last 4 months and I don’t trust myself to remember without proof given my current state. Fuck, it might not have and I’m just going crazy. That’s exactly my point. I don’t even know if I want to work in technology at all anymore. It just seems like it has the potential to hurt people. I don’t even know why I keep this blog other than as another journal I can’t trust.

-Charlie.
