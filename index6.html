MY FUTURE

2/08/2006

I’ve been filling out all my college applications and this whole process makes me want to jump off the Brooklyn Bridge. I want to change the world, not study C++ for the 30th time in my life. Even having to pretend I see myself making a difference through traditional education for these essays makes my brain feel like it’s bleeding. It’s a damn shame there isn’t a job in the world where someone will pay me in all the money and resources I need to program my heart out to try and see what is possible. But what do I know, I’m barely an adult. Sure. Whatever.


-Charlie.

MORE ABOUT SAM

2/06/2006

So part of the reason I’ve been so busy lately is because I’ve been hanging out with Sam a ton solo. Mike has been pretty consistently unavailable, so sometimes Sam just comes back to my place after she’s finished track practice to hang out. She talks a lot more when Mike isn’t around, and I even convinced her to read through the manuals of my Elder Scrolls PC games. She said she wished she could read the game instead of play it, which is pretty classic Sam. Maybe one day she’ll sit down and play with me, I think she’d really like it.
