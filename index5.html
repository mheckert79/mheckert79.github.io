NOSTOLGIA + VIDEO GAMES

1/27/2006

I’ve been playing a ton of MGS2 again in my free time. It seems like I’ve been going back to old favorites a ton recently, I think it’s because of the impending dread of growing up. My favorite part about the MGS series is its ability to walk the line between serious and lighthearted, story and play. RAMBLINGS

1/14/2006

Ever since hanging out with Mike and Sam a few days ago I’ve been feeling so weird about the future. I’m almost upset that I like them so much and that we can’t just have the same friendship we had freshman year forever. Like, I know logically we all grow and change, but I hate to think that we’ll never have a weekend like that again. I wish I could replay it and relive it forever and I’m so afraid of forgetting it. It makes me feel a little less crazy for fantasizing about our lives existing as data after all. I don’t know.


-Charlie.

FRIENDS AGAIN

1/21/2006

Me, Mike, and Sam hung out all together for the first time in what seemed like forever. Mike has been so busy fighting with or avoiding his parents I feel like we never see him anymore, so it was great to kick back in the basement, play GTA, and listen to music for a few hours on a friday night. I think we’ve all been feeling a little disconnected from each other now that we’re in our last semester in high school, and Sam is for sure moving to the west coast for some biology degree. It was just nice to be together again in one place.
