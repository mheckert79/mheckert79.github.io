MELEE + MELEE

2/26/2006

I had Mike over to play one of our all-time favorites today: Smash Brothers Melee. Even though neither of us are big Nintendo heads nowadays, when we were younger, this was all we ever played. I play Fox McCloud, and he plays Falco Lombardi, naturally. I thought this would be a great way to actually reconnect and have some escapist fun, but after losing like 3 games in a row, Mike absolutely blew up at me., which is nothing new. This time it was different though. He was genuinely upset. And it wasn’t about the game. He kept yelling about how I never think about him and it wasn’t even really coherent, but he ended with saying he didn’t even know who I was anymore and how I never even respond to his calls, which doesn’t make sense as he hasn’t called me in weeks. He just stormed out and left me here to feel like shit. I don’t think I did anything wrong, but I can’t help but feel like this is my fault. I know he’s going through some stuff right now, but I don’t know if there’s anything I can do to help him right now other than tell Sam.



-Charlie.
