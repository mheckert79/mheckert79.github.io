EMPTY

8/26/2006

It’s been a while since my last post for obvious reasons. I’ve been trying to log off and forget about what happened to process it all but I just can’t. I’m getting ready to move into my new dorm soon and I just can’t continue to think about this blog or anything that might happen.

What changed?
When did it happen?
Was it in an instant? A slow descent into selfishness?
How does who I am today become that….thing?

He wasn’t me but I know in my heart that he was. All I can do now is hope that knowing what I do now I don’t end up like that. I won’t. I can’t.

I’m sure nobody even reads this anymore and the few who do think I’m insane. At least I’ll know what whatever the fuck happened is immortalized here.

I think this is a good place to leave this blog, as a testament to a future I’m trying to prevent.

-Charlie.
